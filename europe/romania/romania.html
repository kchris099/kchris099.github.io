Romania

Romania united the principalities of Wallachia and Moldavia in 1859 on Dacian and Roman foundations in Transylvania. Ottoman, Habsburg, and Russian neighbours surrounded an Orthodox and Latin cultural core whose castles, painted monasteries, and 20th-century cities led to European Union membership.
