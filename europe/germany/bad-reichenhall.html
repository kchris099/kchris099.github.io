Bad Reichenhall

Bad Reichenhall's salt springs financed medieval trade and later a spa economy in Bavaria's Berchtesgadener Land. Wittelsbach rulers guarded the brine works; 19th-century Kurhaus buildings and saltwater graduation towers turned the town into a resort. Alpine hinterland links to Salzburg and Berchtesgaden shaped traffic. Today the old saltworks museum, spa parks, and mountain railways record how brine wealth and health tourism built the town.
