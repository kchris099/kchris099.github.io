Germany

Germany unified in 1871 from principalities and free cities that had long governed Central Europe. The Holy Roman Empire, Reformation, Hanseatic trade, and industrial growth left castles, cathedrals, town halls, and museums across the Rhine valleys, Alpine foothills, and northern Hanseatic port cities.
