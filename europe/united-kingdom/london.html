London

London grew from a Roman Londinium on the Thames into the capital of the Kingdom of England and later the United Kingdom. Medieval Westminster, Tudor commerce, and Georgian expansion gave way to Victorian industry and 20th-century reconstruction after wartime bombing. The Tower, St Paul's Cathedral, royal parks, and parliamentary institutions show how the city became a global financial and cultural centre.
