Greece Guide

Greece won independence from Ottoman rule in the 19th century, building a modern nation on classical city-states and Byzantine provinces. Temples, agoras, fortified islands, and Orthodox monasteries across the mainland and archipelago trace political change from antiquity through Venetian and Ottoman periods.
