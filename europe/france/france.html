France Guide

France emerged from Roman Gaul through medieval kingdoms centred on Paris and revolutionary upheaval in 1789. Enlightenment planning, imperial expansion, and republican institutions left cathedrals, châteaux, and grands projets from Normandy to the Mediterranean. Regional cuisines, wine routes, and UNESCO-listed quarters reflect how monarchy, revolution, and EU membership shaped European culture.
