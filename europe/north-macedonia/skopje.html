Skopje

Roman and Ottoman Skopje met at the stone bridge and bazaar on the Vardar. The 1963 earthquake flattened much of the centre; reconstruction mixed brutalist civic buildings with restored Ottoman monuments. The city's historic face is that juxtaposition of bazaar and post-quake modernism. Kale fortress above the river anchors the pre-Ottoman and medieval skyline.
