Portugal

Portugal developed as an Atlantic kingdom whose explorers and merchants reshaped world trade from the capture of Ceuta in 1415 onward. Hill towns, monasteries, riverfront quarters, and azulejo-clad civic buildings from Lisbon to the Algarve carry maritime ambition from the Age of Discovery to the modern republic.
