Poznan

An early centre of the Polish state and a medieval fair town on the Warta, Poznań saw its island cathedral and Renaissance town hall rebuilt after 1945 destruction. Trade-fair grounds later added a modern economic layer to that Piast and mercantile core. St. Martin's croissant tradition and the June festival calendar grow from that fair-town culture.
