Poland

Poland's cities preserve royal and burgher architecture from medieval Piast kingdoms, the Polish-Lithuanian Commonwealth, partitions by neighbouring powers, and restored independence in 1918. Market squares, castle districts, and parish churches in Kraków, Warsaw, and Gdańsk trace Central European political and religious change.
