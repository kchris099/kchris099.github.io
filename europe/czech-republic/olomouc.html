Explore Olomouc

A Brief History

Olomouc served as the historical capital of Moravia until the seventeenth century, standing as a primary center of religious and political power in the Czech lands. The city was severely damaged during the Thirty Years' War when occupied by Swedish forces, leading to its subsequent reconstruction in a triumphant Baroque style. As a major fortress city under the Habsburgs and the seat of an archbishopric, it attracted prominent artists and scholars. Today, the city's ecclesiastical wealth is visible in the Holy Trinity Column, Saint Wenceslas Cathedral, and numerous Baroque fountains.

Hradisko Monastery

The Hradisko Monastery, located in the northern part of Olomouc, Czech Republic, is a historic building with a rich and varied past. Originally established as a Benedictine monastery in 1078, it later came under the ownership of the Premonstratensian order in the mid-12th century.

Free

Upper Square

Upper Square, or Horni Namesti, is a must-visit destination for anyone exploring Olomouc. This active square is brimming with architectural marvels that tell the city's rich history. At its heart stands the 15th-century baroque Town Hall, complete with a unique astronomical clock that has an intriguing past shaped by both war and ideology.

Free

Olomouc Astronomical Clock

Olomouc Astronomical Clock is a monument in Olomouc, Czech Republic. Originally constructed in the 1400s, it underwent significant changes after being damaged during World War II. The clock's redesign during the Communist era resulted in unique Social Realist mosaics and an animated display at noon featuring depictions of workers and engineers. The memorial stands in an open plaza or roadside setting.

Free

Church of St. Maurice

The Church of St. Maurice, also known as the St. Moritz Church, is a 13th-century Roman Catholic church in Olomouc featuring Gothic architecture and a stone belltower. It was consecrated at the end of the 14th century, although its exact construction date remains unknown. The church's asymmetrical two towers are an element of Olomouc's skyline, with the southern tower being a popular tourist attraction offering panoramic views of the city when climbed.

Free

Marian Column

Marian Column is a monument in Olomouc, Czech Republic. Records associated with the site include 1723. The Marian Column, erected in 1723 on Dolni namesti, commemorates the city's survival of a devastating plague outbreak. Surrounding it are diverse dining establishments and bars catering to various preferences. The memorial stands in an open plaza or roadside setting.

Andrův stadium

Andrův stadium is a unique venue, featuring residential apartments as part of its stands and a strikingly curved opposing section. This unconventional design creates an authentic Czech football atmosphere that appeals to fans. The stadium is known for offering affordable beer and wurst, adding to the overall enjoyable experience. Despite not being overly spacious, the stadium has beauty and charm that any football enthusiast would appreciate.

CZK 130

Zoo Olomouc

Zoo Olomouc is a visitor site in Olomouc, Czech Republic. This enchanting zoo is home to over 1,800 animals representing more than 354 species from around the globe. With a strong commitment to conservation, it actively participates in breeding programs aimed at protecting endangered species through initiatives like the European Endangered Species Programme (EEP). The grounds and principal structures are the main points of interest.

Free

Unofficial Minor Basilica of the Visitation of the Virgin Mary on Svatý Kopeček

The Minor Basilica of the Visitation of the Virgin Mary on Svatý Kopeček is a piece of baroque architecture that offers panoramic views of Olomouc. Although still undergoing restoration, it remains a must-visit attraction. The interior is adorned with golden details and houses organs. Visitors can enjoy the surroundings and even catch a sunset from this hilltop location, easily accessible by bus.

Free

Saint Wenceslas Cathedral Olomouc

Saint Wenceslas Cathedral Olomouc is a religious building in Olomouc, Czech Republic. Saint Wenceslas Cathedral in Olomouc, Czech Republic is a restored Gothic cathedral dating back to the 12th century. The exterior and main interior spaces are usually viewable outside service times.

CZK 200

Archbishop's Palace

The Archbishop's Palace in Olomouc is a example of early Baroque architecture, located just a short distance from the cathedral. The palace offers guided tours that provide insight into its history, former residents, and notable visitors. Built on the foundations of the old Olomouc Castle, it now houses the city's most important museum and church. Visitors can explore the Archdiocesan Museum to view artifacts dating back 1,000 years.
