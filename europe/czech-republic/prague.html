Explore Prague

A Brief History

Prague, the 'City of a Hundred Spires', is a breathtakingly beautiful European capital completely unharmed by 20th-century wars. Its remarkably preserved medieval core features the spectacular Charles Bridge, winding cobblestone streets, and the massive Prague Castle complex overlooking the Vltava River. It perfectly blends rich Bohemian history, vibrant arts, and the world's finest beer culture.
