Lviv

Polish, Austrian, and Jewish periods layered Lviv without the wholesale wartime erasure that hit many peer cities. Habsburg façades and coffeehouse culture give western Ukraine a distinctly Central European streetscape. The historic centre is valued precisely for that intact multi-imperial fabric. The Armenian cathedral and Latin cathedral share the centre with former Jewish districts.
