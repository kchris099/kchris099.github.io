Destination Guide — Interlaken

Explore Interlaken

A Brief History

Initially developing around an influential 12th-century Augustinian monastery, Interlaken was completely redefined by 19th-century romantic tourism. The arrival of the railways unlocked its dramatic alpine valleys, transforming the quiet settlement into Switzerland's premier adventure hub. Visitors today can clearly trace the intricate layers of this unique heritage throughout the preserved streets and monuments.

Top Sights & Activities

Yash Chopra Statue

The Yash Chopra Statue in Interlaken, Switzerland pays tribute to the renowned Indian film director who extensively used the Swiss Alps as a backdrop for his movies. The bronze statue, weighing over 350 kg, stands in Kursaal Garden and is a gesture of appreciation from the Swiss government for Chopra's role in popularizing the scenic beauty of Switzerland to South Asia.

Free

AlpinAir Paragliding Interlaken

AlpinAir Paragliding Interlaken offers an exhilarating experience with aerial views of Lake Thun and the peaks of Eiger, Monch, and Jungfrau. The descent from around 800m culminates in a central landing in Interlaken. The professional team ensures a seamless and enjoyable adventure, with personalized attention to detail. Passengers rave about the engaging and interactive pilots who provide an ride filled with thrilling maneuvers.

20 CHF

St. Beatus-Höhlen, Swiss Caves

The St. Beatus Caves, located near Interlaken, feature an extensive network of illuminated underground limestone caverns adorned with impressive stalactites and stalagmites. According to legend, the caves were once the lair of a dragon slain by the Irish monk St. Beatus. The site also features a cascading waterfall and a museum detailing the cave's geology and history.

Free

OUTDOOR - Interlaken Base

If you're seeking an adventure in the heart of Switzerland, look no further than Outdoor - Interlaken Base. Nestled between the Lake Thun and Lake Brienz, this destination is a paradise for outdoor enthusiasts. One standout experience is snowshoeing through the Swiss Alps, where traverse picturesque valleys and reach majestic mountain tops.

Free

Harder Kulm

Harder Kulm is a must-visit elevated lookout point in Interlaken, Switzerland, offering views of the surrounding mountains and lakes. It's an ideal destination for families, couples, friends, and solo explorers. The modern building at the top provides seating for 130 people and is perfect for meetings, seminars, and events with its unique view.

Free

Ruine Weissenau

Ruine Weissenau, located near Interlaken, Switzerland, offers a glimpse into the medieval era with its well-preserved ruins dating back to the 13th century. Once a fortified castle overlooking the Aare River, it now provides panoramic views of the surrounding countryside. Visitors can explore its walls and towers while enjoying a pleasant walk from town. The nearby lake adds to the scenic beauty of this historical site.

Free

Thrill Walk

Located in Birg, Switzerland, the Thrill Walk is a heart-pumping experience for adventure seekers. This glass-floored sightseeing path offers views of the Eiger and Jungfrau peaks. The steel platforms and ladders bolted into the cliffs provide a thrilling bird's eye view of the Jungfrau massif. With transparent sections, daring steps, and perspectives, it's an exhilarating adventure for those seeking an adrenaline rush amidst the beautiful Swiss Alps.

Free

Grütschalp - Mürren - Schilthorn - Retour

Grütschalp - Mürren - Schilthorn - Retour offers an experience with views of the Eiger, Monch, and Jungfrau mountains. The journey begins with a cable car ride from Lauterbrunnen to Grütschalp followed by a scenic BLM train ride to Mürren. From there, visitors can enjoy vistas and even ski in winter.

Free

Mürren Viewpoint

Mürren Viewpoint is a captivating mountain village nestled in the Bernese Oberland of Switzerland. It's an ideal destination for those seeking tranquility, especially during the months of April and May when tourist numbers are low. The village, accessible by cable car, offers views of the Eiger, Monch, and Jungfrau peaks. Mürren is a car-free haven with charming chalets, flower-filled meadows, and picturesque hiking trails.

Free

First

First is a destination that offers an array of outdoor adventures and vistas. Nestled in the Bernese Alps, it serves as a popular hiking hub where visitors can embark on a scenic 5.5 km trail leading from First to Grosse Scheidegg. This easy trek takes about two hours and showcases rolling meadows alongside magnificent views of the Jungfrau massif, making every step worthwhile.
