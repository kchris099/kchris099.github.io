Rome

Rome developed from Iron Age settlements on the Palatine and Capitoline hills into the capital of the Roman Republic and Empire, with forums, baths, and aqueducts still spread across the city. After the western empire fell, the papacy shaped medieval and Baroque Rome with churches, palaces, and pilgrim routes. Capture of Rome in 1871 made it capital of unified Italy, adding ministries, monuments, and boulevards.
