Italy Guide

Italy unified as a modern state in 1861, but its regions preserve Roman, medieval, Renaissance, and early modern polities that governed the peninsula for centuries. Archaeological sites, churches, civic squares, and railway corridors in Rome, Florence, and regional capitals trace trade, papal power, and industrial growth.
