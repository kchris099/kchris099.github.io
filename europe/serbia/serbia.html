Serbia

Serbia's towns and fortresses carry medieval Nemanjić kingdoms, Ottoman administration, and modern Balkan state formation. Churches, Belgrade's riverfront quarters, and civic institutions along the Danube and Morava valleys grew as a political, religious, and transport hub between central Europe and the Ottoman world.
