Mexico Guide

Mexico unites Mesoamerican civilizations, Spanish colonial rule from 1521, and independence achieved in 1821 into a federal republic stretching from the Rio Grande to Central America. Aztec and Maya heritage, baroque cathedrals, revolutionary murals, and regional cuisines shape its cities, highland towns, and coastal resorts.
