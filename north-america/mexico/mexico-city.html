Mexico City

Mexico City stands on the site of Tenochtitlan, the Aztec capital conquered in 1521 and rebuilt by Spanish planners around the Zócalo. As capital of New Spain and later independent Mexico, it absorbed baroque churches, 19th-century reforms, and rapid 20th-century growth across the Valley of Mexico. Chapultepec, the historic centre, and surrounding boroughs record layered indigenous, colonial, and modern civic life.
