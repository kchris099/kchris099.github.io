Monteverde

Monteverde was settled in the 1950s by American Quakers who sought a peaceful, agricultural lifestyle and successfully preserved large tracts of the surrounding cloud forest to protect their turning point. This initial conservation effort laid the foundation for the Monteverde Cloud Forest Reserve, established in 1972, which became a global model for ecotourism and biodiversity research. The community shifted from dairy farming to sustainable tourism over the decades.
