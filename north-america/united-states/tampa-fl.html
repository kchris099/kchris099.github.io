Explore Tampa, FL

A Brief History

Tampa grew from a U.S. Army post at Fort Brooke on Tampa Bay into a cigar-manufacturing centre after Vicente Martinez Ybor moved factories from Cuba in 1885. Ybor City brick cigar halls, Bayshore boulevard mansions, and port cruise terminals record how Spanish and Italian immigration, phosphate shipping, and tourism reshaped a Gulf Coast harbour. Walking routes through the historic centre help visitors relate these monuments to wider patterns of settlement and civic life in United States.

Top Sights & Activities

Tampa Theatre

Tampa Theatre, a historic movie palace built in 1926, is a must-visit cultural attraction in Florida. Recognized as theaters globally, it hosts over 600 events annually, including films, concerts, and festivals. The landmark boasts old-world features and a Wurlitzer organ.

Free

Straz Center for the Performing Arts

The Straz Center for the Performing Arts is a prominent venue in the Southeastern United States, offering a 4,327-seat space for dance, theater, and opera performances. Situated on the Hillsborough River, it hosts over 4,000 concerts and events annually, including grand opera and Broadway shows. The center is also home to the Patel Conservatory which provides educational programs and workshops in performing arts for educators, families, and adults.

$39

The Florida Aquarium

Embark on an journey at The Florida Aquarium, nestled in the heart of downtown Tampa. This destination is a treasure trove for families and marine enthusiasts alike, offering a plethora of interactive experiences that cater to all ages. From thrilling encounters with sharks and playful penguins to hands-on stingray touch tanks, every corner of the aquarium invites exploration and wonder.

Free

Sparkman Wharf

Sparkman Wharf is a trendy waterfront destination that offers a variety of dining options, shops, and entertainment. The area features a atmosphere with live music, waterfront views, and misted fans for warmer days. It's home to concepts from acclaimed chefs and offers diverse culinary experiences. Visitors can enjoy outdoor activities such as watching sports games or participating in yoga sessions on the lawn.

Free

Bass Pro Shops

Bass Pro Shops is a well-known chain of large, wilderness-themed stores offering a wide range of hunting, fishing, and outdoor gear. The store hosts free events such as fishing at their nearby pond and crafting activities. Visitors praise the cleanliness, organization, and professional staff who cater to both amateurs and professionals with a diverse selection of items for fishing, hunting, and more.

Free

Wat Mongkolratanaram Buddhist Temple

Wat Mongkolratanaram Buddhist Temple in Tampa offers a unique cultural experience, showcasing the beauty of Thai architecture and providing insight into the local community. The temple serves as a support center and hosts a popular Sunday market featuring authentic Thai cuisine. Visitors can explore the grounds, enjoy traditional music, and shop for orchids and bonsai trees.

$60

Busch Gardens Tampa Bay

Busch Gardens Tampa Bay is a renowned amusement park in Tampa, Florida, offering a wide range of activities for visitors of all ages. Operated by SeaWorld Entertainment, the park features thrilling rides, live entertainment, and special attractions for kids. In addition to the adrenaline-pumping roller coasters like Iron Gwazi, visitors can also enjoy unique experiences such as Serengeti Safari and Guided Adventure Tours.

$60

ZooTampa at Lowry Park

ZooTampa at Lowry Park, located in North Tampa, Florida, is a renowned zoo that offers an exciting and educational experience for visitors of all ages. The zoo provides opportunities for up-close encounters with a diverse range of animals from around the globe, including big cats, primates, reptiles, and birds. With its commitment to conservation and education, ZooTampa at Lowry Park aims to raise awareness about the importance of protecting wildlife and their habitats.

Free

MIDFLORIDA Credit Union Amphitheatre

The MidFlorida Credit Union Amphitheatre, also known as the Amp, is a popular outdoor venue located at the Florida State Fairgrounds. It has 9,900 covered seats and an outdoor lawn that can accommodate 10,000 people. The venue offers free parking for self-parking and also provides valet and premium parking options. Starting in May, the amphitheater will host a variety of shows featuring big names in comedy and music.

Free

Centro Ybor

Centro Ybor is a 2-floor mall In Tampa's historic Latin district, Ybor City. It offers a diverse range of attractions including food outlets, bars, a 10-screen cinema, theater, stores, and offices. While it primarily serves as a dining and entertainment complex, visitors can also explore retail shops and witness artisans making hand-rolled cigars at the Columbia Restaurant Cigar Store.

Free

Centro Asturiano de Tampa

Centro Asturiano de Tampa, established in 1902, is a historic Spanish social club and events venue listed on the National Register of Historic Places. The original building was destroyed in 1912, but a new neoclassical-style structure was erected to replace it. The venue offers a glimpse into Tampa's past with its gymnasium, cantina, billiard room, library, theater, and more.
