Boise, ID

Oregon Trail traffic paused at a tree-lined river crossing that French-speakers called Boisé. Capital status after 1890, Basque boarding houses, and Boise River irrigation projects shaped a high-desert administrative city between Rockies and Snake River plain. The Basque Block restaurants and museum recognise a diaspora drawn by sheep work. The Idaho State Capitol's sandstone dome anchors the downtown mall axis.
