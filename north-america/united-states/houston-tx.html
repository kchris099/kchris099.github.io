Houston, TX

Houston was founded in 1836 by New York land promoters near Buffalo Bayou's port to the Gulf of Mexico. Ship Channel refineries, Museum District institutions, and NASA Johnson Space Center campuses record how cotton export, Spindletop oil discoveries, and postwar petrochemical investment built America's fourth-largest city on a humid coastal plain.
