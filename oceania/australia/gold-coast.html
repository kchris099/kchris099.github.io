Destination Guide — Gold Coast

Explore Gold Coast

A Brief History

Established as a regional settlement beyond an older fortified core, the city developed through industrialization in the late 19th century, post-war reconstruction, and modern civic expansion that reshaped its public spaces, markets, and surrounding landscape. Restored religious buildings, civic buildings, and natural features now define the contemporary visitor experience.

Top Sights & Activities

Free

Harbour Town Premium Outlets

Harbour Town Premium Outlets is Australia's largest outlet shopping center, located in Biggera Waters, just a 30-minute drive from Surfers Paradise. It boasts over 240 stores offering up to 70% off on big-name brands like Coach, Ralph Lauren, Victoria's Secret, Hugo Boss, Calvin Klein, Nike and more. travellers can also enjoy casual eateries and a 14-screen cinema. The center provides a free Outlet Shopping Shuttle seven days a week for easy access.

Free

SkyPoint Observation Deck

Located on the 77th and 78th levels of the Q1 building, SkyPoint Observation Deck offers panoramic views of the Gold Coast and its hinterland. As Australia's only beachside observation deck, it stands at a remarkable height of 230 meters. travellers can take in the scenery through high-power binoculars and visual displays that highlight key points of interest in the region.

Free

Timezone Surfers Paradise

TimeZone Surfers Paradise is a massive indoor family theme park in Australia, offering an array of interactive games, activities, and attractions. It's the largest indoor family entertainment center in the country and has recently reopened with even more exciting features. From laser tag to glow-in-the-dark mini golf, bumper cars, arcade games, and more than 350 other games and activities, there's something for everyone here.

Free

Surfers Paradise Beachfront Markets

The Surfers Paradise Beachfront Markets are a popular open-air night market held every Wednesday, Friday, and Saturday from 4pm to 9pm. With over 100 stalls offering a variety of items such as handmade jewelry, locally produced goods, and even the opportunity to make wax hand molds as souvenirs, there's something for everyone. The markets have been an icon of Surfers Paradise for over 25 years and offer a low-key shopping experience with gourmet treats and local flavors.

Free

Pacific Fair Shopping Centre

Pacific Fair Shopping Centre is a massive retail destination In the Gold Coast, near Jupiter's Hotel and Casino. It features an extensive range of brand-name department stores, boutiques, services, and dining options. The center also includes Australia's first purpose-built outlet shopping precinct within a major regional shopping center. travellers can find great bargains from renowned brands like Polo Ralph Lauren, Calvin Klein, Nike, and more.

Free

GC Aqua Park

Situated in the scenic Broadwater Parklands on the Gold Coast, GC Aqua Park is an expansive inflatable water park offering endless excitement for travellers of all ages. This family-friendly destination features thrilling water slides, entertaining obstacles, and vast inflatable playgrounds against a backdrop of beautiful beaches. Open year-round, it provides an accessible and budget-friendly option for creating cherished memories with loved ones.

Free

Sea World

Sea World is an oceanographic theme park that offers a variety of attractions for travellers of all ages. Guests can watch polar bears at Polar Bear Shores, observe tropical fish and sharks in the world's largest man-made shark lagoon, and even touch rays at Ray Reef. The park also features entertaining seal presentations and live dolphin shows.

Free

Hinze Dam

Hinze Dam, located in the hinterland of the Gold Coast, is a massive structure offering views of Advancetown Lake, surrounding mountains, and the spillway. The area features popular hiking routes like Mount Warning Summit Trail, Tallebudgera Creek Circuit, and Burleigh Headland Coastal Walk. It's also a serene location for wedding photography with its tranquil lake surface surrounded by lush vegetation and rolling hills.

Free

Wet'n'Wild Gold Coast

Wet'n'Wild Gold Coast is an expansive water park located just 1km from Movie World theme park, making it convenient to visit both attractions in one day. The park offers a variety of thrill rides, a wave pool, concessions, and a surf shop. It's perfect for families or groups of friends looking for fun activities.

Free

Australian Outback Spectacular

Australian Outback is a captivating bush-themed dinner show set in a high-tech arena that offers an immersive experience into the heartland of Australia. The show follows the story of struggling farmers Reg and Marge, showcasing the challenges and resilience of outback life. Guests are treated to a three-course meal supporting local farmers while being entertained by horseback riding, cattle mustering, music, and impressive special effects.

Free

Burleigh Head National Park

Burleigh Head National Park is a surprising gem in Burleigh Heads, offering ocean views, rainforest trails, and wildlife encounters. The coastal headland area provides an easy escape into nature with its rocky foreshore, ancient volcanic columns, and lush mangroves. travellers can enjoy picnics or barbecues amidst fragrant pine trees while spotting whales during spring. The park features well-maintained walking tracks for exploring the diverse landscapes and relaxing by the ocean.
