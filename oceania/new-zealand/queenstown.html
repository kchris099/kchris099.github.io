Destination Guide — Queenstown

Explore Queenstown

A Brief History

Born from the chaotic frenzy of the 1862 Shotover River gold rush, Queenstown was a booming frontier town that eventually emptied as the gold ran out. The majestic alpine scenery later sparked a massive revival, redefining the region as the adventure capital of the Southern Hemisphere. The remarkable survival of these cultural and architectural milestones provides a powerful window into the region's storied past.

Top Sights & Activities

Free

Skyline Queenstown

Skyline Queenstown offers an experience in Queenstown, New Zealand. Visitors can take the Queenstown Gondola up 450 meters to Bob's Peak for panoramic views of The Remarkables mountain range and Lake Wakatipu. At the top, they can enjoy dining at Market Kitchen Cafe or Stratosfare Restaurant, and even visit the Jelly Belly Store for some sweet treats and an art gallery featuring pieces made out of jelly beans.

Free

iFLY Indoor Skydiving Queenstown

iFLY Queenstown offers the exhilarating experience of indoor skydiving in a state-of-the-art vertical wind tunnel. The facility allows visitors of all ages to experience the sensation of freefall in a safe, controlled environment, guided by professional instructors. It provides a thrilling, accessible adventure activity in the heart of the 'Adventure Capital of the World.'

Free

Shotover Jet Queenstown

Shotover Jet is a world-famous, high-speed jet boat ride operating on the Shotover River near Queenstown. The powerful boats navigate the narrow, dramatic canyons of the river at thrilling speeds, performing 360-degree spins and close passes to the rocky cliff walls. It is one of the most iconic and enduring adventure tourism attractions in New Zealand.

Free

Onsen Hot Pools and Day Spa

Nestled in Arthurs Point, just a short drive from Queenstown, Onsen Hot Pools Retreat and Day Spa offers an exclusive and luxurious experience. The private cedar-lined hot tubs provide views of the Shotover River canyon, making it a perfect romantic getaway. While the cost may be on the higher side, the experience is well worth it for couples looking to indulge in a unique and intimate retreat.

Free

Tiki Trail- Top Station

Embark on an exhilarating adventure along the Tiki Trail, a challenging yet rewarding hiking path that leads you from Queenstown to Bob's Peak. This scenic route winds through lush Douglas fir and beech forests, offering views of Lake Wakatipu and the Remarkables mountain range. The trail is steep, providing a solid workout that typically takes 1 to 2 hours to conquer.

Free

Thrillzone Queenstown

Thrillzone Queenstown is an indoor and outdoor adventure center offering a variety of activities for all ages. From virtual reality gaming and escape rooms to mini-golf and go-karts, there's something for everyone. The state-of-the-art multiplayer virtual reality systems provide an immersive experience, while the minigolf course, escape rooms, laser tag, and paintball arenas offer classic entertainment. Visitors can also enjoy a captivating 12D movie experience.

Free

TSS Earnslaw - RealNZ

The TSS Earnslaw is a historic, twin-screw coal-fired steamboat that has been operating on Lake Wakatipu since 1912. Known as the 'Lady of the Lake,' it offers scenic cruises from Queenstown, providing passengers with a nostalgic journey and dramatic alpine views. Visitors can also view the engine room and the stokers at work.

Free

Queenstown Hill Walking Track

The Queenstown Hill Walking Track is a popular hiking trail that offers panoramic views of the lake and mountains as it traverses hills and forests. Also known as the Time Walk or Queenstown Millennium Time Walk, this moderately challenging hike takes approximately 2 to 3 hours to complete. Along the way, hikers can learn about the area's history through information plaques.

Free

AJ Hackett Bungy - Queenstown Bungy Centre

AJ Hackett Bungy - Queenstown Bungy Centre offers an exhilarating experience for adrenaline junkies. With a focus on safety and fun, the professional crew ensures that visitors leave with memories. The center provides various thrilling activities including bungy jumping, swings, catapult, and zipline. Additionally, visitors can enjoy cycling to the center and surprise their kids with a jump. The site also features a cafe for relaxation after the adrenaline rush.

Free

Ziptrek Ecotours

Ziptrek Ecotours offers guided zipline adventures through the ancient native beech forest on Bobs Peak, above Queenstown. The tours feature a series of thrilling ziplines interspersed with educational commentary on the local ecology and conservation efforts. The elevated course provides unique, panoramic views of the town, Lake Wakatipu, and the Remarkables mountain range.
