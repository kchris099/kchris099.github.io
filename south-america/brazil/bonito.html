Explore Bonito

A Brief History

A quiet ranching community for much of its history, Bonito's transformation began in the late 20th century when its crystal-clear rivers and limestone caves were recognized. Strict environmental controls have since shaped it into Brazil's premier ecotourism destination. The remarkable survival of these cultural and architectural milestones provides a powerful window into the region's storied past.

Top Sights & Activities

Free

Gruta do Lago Azul Natural Monument

Gruta do Lago Azul Natural Monument is a destination in Bonito, known for its cavernous space adorned with stalactites and stalagmites. The highlight of the monument is the transparent lake with vibrant blue water at the bottom of the cavern. Visitors can explore various natural attractions such as rivers, lakes, crystal clear pools, and diverse marine life.

Free

Grutas de São Miguel Dia e Noite

Grutas de São Miguel is one of the notable caves in Bonito, along with Gruta Sao Mateus and Gruta do Lago Azul. The latter features a sapphire and tourmaline lake 330 feet below the surface, making it water-filled caves on Earth. The natural monument is protected under conservation statuses, offering limited tours daily.

Free

Balneario Municipal, Bonito

Bonito Municipal Balneary is a popular spot for both locals and tourists, offering a relaxed atmosphere and activities for the whole family. Unlike other attractions in Bonito, it allows visitors to explore at their own pace without a guide. Situated along the Rio Formoso, this balneary features crystal-clear waters perfect for swimming and observing fish. The facilities include restrooms, parking, snack bars, restaurants, and even wheelchair-accessible amenities.

Free

Natural Aquarium

Natural Aquarium sits within the Balneario Municipal, Bonito area of Bonito, documented in local heritage registers for its civic or cultural role. Visitors approach from the main access road and combine the visit with neighbouring heritage buildings and public spaces in the district.

Free

Liberty Square

Liberty Square (Plaza de la Libertad) is a modern public space in Taipei, Taiwan, featuring extensive green areas, water features, and contemporary art installations. It serves as a popular venue for cultural events, open-air concerts, and community gatherings, providing a vibrant urban oasis amidst the bustling commercial district.

Free

Bonito's aquarium

Tucked away in a residential area, Bonito's Aquarium is a hidden gem that offers an engaging experience focused on environmental education. This indoor aquarium showcases a variety of local species, including the fascinating piranha. Visitors can enjoy guided tours led by enthusiastic staff who share intriguing facts about the freshwater fish native to Brazil’s rivers. One of the highlights includes feeding fish with food on a stick and even getting up close with freshwater stingrays!.

Free

Projeto Jibóia

Established in 2005, Projeto Jibóia is a center dedicated to educating visitors about non-venomous snakes and their behaviors. Founded by Henry Naufal, the project aims to promote knowledge about the region's snake species in a fun and engaging way, dispelling negative perceptions and combating illegal hunting.

Free

Bio Park

Bio Park is a unique wildlife sanctuary that provides a safe haven for animals rescued from various situations such as abuse, wildfires, accidents, and illegal animal trade. The park was established three years ago with the noble intention of addressing the scarcity of animal shelters nationwide. It currently houses over 20 species of local wildlife.

Free

Parque das Cachoeiras

Nestled in the heart of Bonito, Parque das Cachoeiras is a must-visit destination for nature lovers and adventure seekers alike. This park features seven picturesque waterfalls, each offering unique spots for swimming and relaxation amidst the scenery. The ecological trail meanders through 400 hectares of lush landscapes, including 350 hectares of preserved forest teeming with diverse flora and fauna.

Free

Praia da Figueira

Nestled about 19 kilometers from the heart of Bonito, MS, Praia da Figueira is a delightful lakeside resort that boasts an expansive stretch of white sandy beach alongside a lagoon measuring 60,000 square meters. This picturesque destination invites visitors to unwind in nature while indulging in various activities such as kayaking, pedal boating, and even zip-lining. The crystal-clear waters are perfect for swimming or simply relaxing by the shore.
