Explore Bonito

A Brief History

Bonito is in Brazil. Strict environmental controls have since shaped it into Brazil's premier ecotourism destination. The survival of these cultural and architectural milestones provides a powerful window into the region's storied past. Museums, markets, and street plans in Bonito still show layers from those periods. Administrative and transport changes in later centuries reshaped the town plan of Bonito.

Top Sights & Activities

Gruta do Lago Azul Natural Monument

Gruta do Lago Azul Natural Monument is a destination in Bonito, known for its cavernous space adorned with stalactites and stalagmites. The highlight of the monument is the transparent lake with active blue water at the bottom of the cavern. Visitors can explore various natural attractions such as rivers, lakes, clear pools, and diverse marine life.

R$143

Grutas de São Miguel Dia e Noite

Grutas de São Miguel Dia e Noite is a visitor site in Bonito, Brazil. Grutas de São Miguel is one of the notable caves in Bonito, along with Gruta Sao Mateus and Gruta do Lago Azul. The natural monument is protected under conservation statuses, offering limited tours daily. The grounds and principal structures are the main points of interest.

R$98

Balneario Municipal, Bonito

Balneario Municipal, Bonito is a visitor site in Bonito, Brazil. Bonito Municipal Balneary is a popular spot for both locals and tourists, offering a relaxed atmosphere and activities for the whole family. Unlike other attractions in Bonito, it allows visitors to explore at their own pace without a guide. The grounds and principal structures are the main points of interest.

R$436

Natural Aquarium

Natural Aquarium sits within the Balneario Municipal, Bonito area of Bonito, documented in local heritage registers for its civic or cultural role. Visitors approach from the main access road and combine the visit with neighbouring heritage buildings and public spaces in the district.

Free

Liberty Square

Liberty Square is a square in Bonito, Brazil. Liberty Square (Plaza de la Libertad) is a modern public space in Taipei, Taiwan, featuring extensive green areas, water features, and contemporary art installations. Open pavement and surrounding facades frame the square.

R$436

Bonito's aquarium

Bonito's aquarium is a visitor site in Bonito, Brazil. This indoor aquarium showcases a variety of local species, including the fascinating piranha. Visitors can enjoy guided tours led by enthusiastic staff who share intriguing facts about the freshwater fish native to Brazil's rivers. One of the highlights includes feeding fish with food on a stick and even getting up close with freshwater stingrays! The grounds and principal structures are the main points of interest.

R$80

Projeto Jibóia

Established in 2005, Projeto Jibóia is a center dedicated to educating visitors about non-venomous snakes and their behaviors. Founded by Henry Naufal, the project aims to promote knowledge about the region's snake species in a fun and engaging way, dispelling negative perceptions and combating illegal hunting.

R$105

Bio Park

Bio Park is a unique wildlife sanctuary that provides a safe haven for animals rescued from various situations such as abuse, wildfires, accidents, and illegal animal trade. The park was established three years ago with the noble intention of addressing the scarcity of animal shelters nationwide. It currently houses over 20 species of local wildlife.

R$312

Parque das Cachoeiras

Parque das Cachoeiras is a visitor site in Bonito, Brazil. The ecological trail meanders through 400 hectares of lush landscapes, including 350 hectares of preserved forest teeming with diverse flora and fauna. The grounds and principal structures are the main points of interest.

R$139

Praia da Figueira

Located about 19 kilometers from the Bonito, MS, Praia da Figueira is a lakeside resort that has an expansive stretch of white sandy beach alongside a lagoon measuring 60,000 square meters. This destination invites visitors to unwind in nature while indulging in various activities such as kayaking, pedal boating, and even zip-lining. The clear waters are perfect for swimming or simply relaxing by the shore.
