Explore Jericoacoara

A Brief History

For generations an isolated fishing hamlet hidden behind massive sand dunes, Jericoacoara operated without electricity until the late 20th century. Its designation as an environmental protection area preserved its rustic coastal identity even as it became a global kite-surfing hub. Visitors today can clearly trace the intricate layers of this unique heritage throughout the preserved streets and monuments.

Top Sights & Activities

Free

Tree Sloth

Jericoacoara, located on the coast of Ceara, has become one of the top holiday destinations in Brazil, attracting a growing number of tourists. This small village exudes a rustic charm and is surrounded by beaches, great restaurants, and a very pleasant atmosphere. The bicycle route from Vila de Jericoacoara to Guriu offers a challenging and scenic journey through the Jericoacoara National Park along the seaside, passing through diverse landscapes.

Free

Lagoa do Paraíso

Lagoa do Paraíso is a impressive freshwater lagoon located near Jericoacoara, famous for its crystal-clear, multi-hued blue waters and white sand beaches. The lagoon offers a tranquil setting for swimming, stand-up paddleboarding, and relaxing in the iconic over-water hammocks. It is a major attraction in the region, providing a serene contrast to the coastal ocean beaches.

Free

Paradise Lagoon

Paradise Lagoon is a documented coastal feature in Jericoacoara, Brazil, recognised in regional geographic listings for its natural formation and public access. The shoreline is open to visitors throughout the year and connects to adjacent beachfront districts. Travellers approach from the coastal road or boardwalk and combine the visit with nearby harbour areas and seaside markets.

Free

Pedra Furada

Pedra Furada is a arch-shaped rock formation located on the picturesque beaches of Jericoacoara, Brazil. This natural wonder offers incredible photo opportunities against the backdrop of towering dunes that rival even those found in the Sahara Desert, with some reaching heights of over 40 meters (130 feet). The journey to Pedra Furada begins at Jericoacoara Beach and takes you through Forro Street and into the heart of the village before leading up Morro do Serrote.

Free

Praça de Jericoacoara

Praça de Jericoacoara, also known as Pracinha de Jeri, is a delightful gathering spot that opened its doors in 2012. This charming square features canoe-shaped benches and two thatched kiosks, creating a cozy ambiance perfect for relaxation. Surrounded by excellent dining options, it has quickly become a popular meeting point in the vibrant village of Jeri.

Free

Dune Sunset Jericoacoara

Dune Sunset Jericoacoara is a popular spot where hundreds of people gather every evening to witness the sunset over the ocean. Tourists and locals alike climb this flat-topped sand dune just before sunset, eagerly anticipating the natural display. The pleasant climate year-round makes it an ideal location for enjoying this daily spectacle.

Free

Praia da Malhada

Praia da Malhada is a picturesque beach with tranquil surroundings and unique rock formations, including the famous Pedra Furada. The rocky area offers an walking experience, especially during low tide when visitors can explore caves and natural pools. Located within walking distance from Jijoca de Jericoacoara, this beach is also close to natural attractions like Dunas do Por Sol and Praia de Jericoacoara.

Free

Jericoacoara National Park

Jericoacoara National Park protects a diverse coastal ecosystem in Ceará, encompassing massive sand dunes, pristine beaches, and clear freshwater lagoons. Established in 2002, the park preserves the unique natural environment and restricts vehicle access to maintain its ecological integrity. Visitors can explore the landscape via dune buggies, horseback, or hiking trails.

Free

Lagoa do Amâncio

The magnificent Lagoa do Amâncio, a aquatic oasis nestled in nature's embrace, captivates visitors with its awe-inspiring beauty and celestial charm. This galactic swimming lake, ensconced within the heart of pristine surroundings, offers an otherworldly experience to those seeking respite from the hustle and bustle of daily life.

Free

Praia do Preá

Praia do Preá is a long, expansive beach located near Jericoacoara, renowned internationally as a premier destination for kitesurfing. The consistent and strong trade winds create ideal conditions for the sport, attracting enthusiasts of all skill levels. The beach maintains a relaxed, rustic atmosphere, with traditional fishing boats and small beachfront pousadas.
