Brazil

Portugal claimed Brazil in 1500 and ruled it as a monarchy until the republic was declared in 1889. Coffee and rubber booms, African and European migration, and 20th-century urban growth left baroque churches in Salvador, planned avenues in Brasília, and a layered Atlantic society across five centuries of plantation economy.
