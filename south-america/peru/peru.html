Peru

Peru was the core of the Inca Empire before Spanish conquest in the 1530s made Lima the viceregal capital of South America's Pacific coast. Independence in 1821 left a republic whose Inca fortresses, colonial plazas, and museums in coastal cities and Andean highlands carry one of the continent's deepest indigenous and Hispanic layers.
