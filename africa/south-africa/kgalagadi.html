Kgalagadi

San hunter-gatherers used the arid Kgalagadi for millennia before colonial borders split the dune country. The 1999 Kgalagadi Transfrontier Park joined South African and Botswanan conservation areas. Wildlife migrations, not civic monuments, are the historical subject here. Red dune streets and dry riverbeds structure game drives more than any built civic core.

Liliesleaf

Liliesleaf Farm in Rivonia, near Sandton, is a very significant heritage site in South African history. During the 1960s, it served as the secret headquarters for the African National Congress (ANC) and other liberation movements. Today, it is an interactive museum detailing the events leading up to the historic 1963 police raid and the subsequent Rivonia Trial.
