Destination Guide — Kgalagadi

Explore Kgalagadi

A Brief History

For millennia, the harsh, arid expanse of the Kgalagadi supported resilient nomadic San hunter-gatherers. The creation of the massive transfrontier park in 1999 united South African and Botswanan conservation efforts, preserving an ancient wilderness largely untouched by modern farming. This continuous historical development established a lasting framework that strongly informs the area's modern cultural evolution.

Top Sights & Activities

Free

The Big Red Barn

The Big Red Barn is a popular, family-oriented outdoor recreation venue located on a large farm near Centurion. It offers a wide variety of activities, including extensive mountain biking and trail running tracks, a treetop zipline course, and a relaxed cafe. The rustic, open-air environment makes it a favorite weekend destination for active families.

Free

Modderfontein Reserve

The Modderfontein Reserve is a large, privately managed conservation area located in the eastern suburbs of Johannesburg. The reserve features diverse habitats, including grasslands, wetlands, and forests, supporting abundant birdlife and small mammals. It offers well-maintained trails for hiking and cycling, as well as picnic areas, providing a peaceful natural retreat within the urban sprawl.

Free

Castle Kyalami - Church of Scientology in South Africa

Castle Kyalami is a prominent, castle-like structure located in the northern suburbs of Johannesburg. Originally built as a luxury hotel and residence, the expansive property was acquired by the Church of Scientology and now serves as their advanced religious retreat and African headquarters. The striking building is a very visible landmark in the Kyalami area.

Free

Beaulieu Bird Sanctuary

The Beaulieu Bird Sanctuary is a small, protected wetland area located within the Beaulieu equestrian estate in Midrand. The sanctuary features a large dam and bird hides, providing a quiet, secure habitat for a wide variety of resident and migratory waterbirds. It is a popular, peaceful spot for local birdwatchers and nature photographers.

Free

Lory Park Zoo

Lory Park Zoo is a small, intimate animal sanctuary and educational facility located in Midrand. It focuses on close-up animal encounters and conservation education, housing a variety of species including big cats, primates, and exotic birds. The zoo is particularly popular with families and school groups, offering interactive experiences like owl feeding and guided tours.

Free

Waterfall City Park

Waterfall City Park is a large, modern urban park located within the expansive Waterfall City development in Midrand. The park features manicured lawns, contemporary landscaping, a large musical fountain, and numerous walking paths. It serves as a central recreational and social hub for the surrounding commercial and residential areas, frequently hosting outdoor events.

Free

Madhya Kailash Temple

Madhya Kailash Temple is a prominent Hindu temple located in Midrand, dedicated to Lord Shiva. The temple complex features traditional Dravidian architecture, with a brightly colored, intricately carved gopuram (entrance tower). It serves as a significant spiritual and cultural center for the local Hindu community, hosting regular religious ceremonies and festivals.

Free

Liliesleaf

Liliesleaf Farm in Rivonia, near Sandton, is a very significant heritage site in South African history. During the 1960s, it served as the secret headquarters for the African National Congress (ANC) and other liberation movements. Today, it is an interactive museum detailing the events leading up to the historic 1963 police raid and the subsequent Rivonia Trial.

Free

Makhulong Park

Makhulong Park is a public recreational facility located in the township of Tembisa, near Johannesburg. The park provides essential green space for the local community, featuring sports fields, children's play areas, and open lawns. It serves as a popular venue for local soccer matches, community gatherings, and weekend relaxation.

Free

Ivory Regional Park

Ivory Regional Park is a large municipal park situated in Ivory Park, a township near Midrand. The park offers extensive open spaces, sports facilities, and picnic areas, catering to the recreational needs of the densely populated surrounding community. It is a vital public space for outdoor activities and social events.
