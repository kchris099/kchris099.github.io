Explore Mount Fuji

A Brief History

Mount Fuji, Japan's tallest and most sacred peak, is a perfectly symmetrical, globally iconic stratovolcano that has profoundly inspired artists and pilgrims for centuries. Surrounded by the breathtaking Fuji Five Lakes, the region offers incredibly picturesque landscapes that dramatically change with the seasons, from vibrant spring cherry blossoms to striking autumn foliage. Whether viewing its majestic snow-capped cone from a serene lakeside park or exploring the traditional, crystal-clear spring villages at its base, the Mount Fuji area delivers absolute, unparalleled natural grandeur.
