Fukuoka

Fukuoka developed around Hakata Bay as a port facing the Korean Peninsula and mainland Asia. The medieval Hakata merchant district traded ceramics and textiles. Nearby Dazaifu served as Kyushu's administrative center in the 8th century. Fukuoka Castle was built in the early 17th century for the Kuroda clan. Modern boulevards and subway lines grew after 1889 city incorporation. Annual festivals such as Hakata Gion Yamakasa date from centuries of urban tradition.
