Naha

Naha is the capital of Okinawa Prefecture on the main island of the Ryukyu chain. Shuri Castle served as the royal seat of the Ryukyu Kingdom from the 15th century until annexation by Japan in 1879. The city was heavily damaged in the 1945 Battle of Okinawa. Postwar U.S. Administration lasted until 1972. Kokusai Street and public markets reflect rebuilding and trade with mainland Japan and East Asia.
