Kyrgyzstan

Kyrgyzstan lies south of the Tian Shan, where Turkic nomads, Silk Road caravans, and Russian garrisons met before Soviet rule from 1922 and independence in 1991. Alpine pastures, felt yurts, and planned cities now anchor a republic built around hydroelectric power and highland tourism.
