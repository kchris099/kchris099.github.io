Sri Lanka

Sri Lanka's island held Sinhalese and Tamil kingdoms, Portuguese and Dutch fortified ports, and British tea-planter hill towns in the Indian Ocean. Anuradhapura stupas, Kandy's Temple of the Tooth, and Galle's ramparts carry Buddhist mission culture, cinnamon trade, and independence in 1948.
