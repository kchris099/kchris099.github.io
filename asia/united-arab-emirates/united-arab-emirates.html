United Arab Emirates

The United Arab Emirates formed as a federation of seven emirates in 1971 whose coastal cities grew from pearling and maritime trade into modern ports and business districts. Historic creek quarters, forts, and planned districts in Dubai and Abu Dhabi reflect treaty-era settlement and hydrocarbon wealth along the Persian Gulf.
