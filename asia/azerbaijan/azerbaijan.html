Azerbaijan

Azerbaijan sits between the Caucasus and the Caspian Sea, where Silk Road caravans, Zoroastrian fire temples, and early oil drilling preceded Russian and Soviet rule. Baku's walled old city, mosque quarters, and waterfront boulevards reflect Persian, imperial, and post-Soviet chapters along the Absheron coast.
