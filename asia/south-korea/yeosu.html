Yeosu

Yeosu has a long maritime history, serving as a important naval base during the Joseon Dynasty. It was the headquarters of Admiral Yi Sun-sin, who famously defended Korea against Japanese invasions in the sixteenth century using the innovative turtle ships. In the modern era, Yeosu developed into a major industrial port and hosted the 2012 World Expo, which revitalized its coastal infrastructure. Today, the city is known for its scenic archipelago, historic pavilions, and active seafood culture.
