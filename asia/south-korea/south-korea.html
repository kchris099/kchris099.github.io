South Korea

South Korea emerged from the peninsula's division after 1945 and the war of 1950–1953, with Seoul as its political and cultural centre through monarchy, Japanese colonisation, reconstruction, and industrial growth. Royal palaces, Buddhist temples, and modern towers continue to shape public space in the capital and beyond.
