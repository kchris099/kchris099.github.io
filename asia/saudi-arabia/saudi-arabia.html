Saudi Arabia

Saudi Arabia unified in 1932 from Bedouin confederations, Wahhabi reform, and desert trade routes across the Arabian Peninsula. Diriyah mud-brick quarters, Jeddah's Red Sea corniche, and Riyadh's modern districts reflect pre-Islamic caravan cities, early Muslim holy sites, and 20th-century oil-era growth.
