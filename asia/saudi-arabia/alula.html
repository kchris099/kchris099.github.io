AlUla Fort Viewpoint

The AlUla Fort Viewpoint offers a commanding, panoramic perspective over the ancient mud-brick ruins of AlUla Old Town and the surrounding lush oasis valley. The elevated vantage point allows visitors to appreciate the strategic location of the historic settlement, located between dramatic sandstone mountains and the fertile palm groves that sustained it for centuries.

03 AdmissionFree

AlUla Tantora

AlUla Tantora is a visitor site in Alula, Saudi Arabia. The Tantora is a historic sundial located in the Old Town of AlUla. For centuries, it served as an important agricultural tool for the local community, marking the start of the winter planting season and regulating the distribution of water from the oasis springs. The main structures and grounds are the principal points of interest.
