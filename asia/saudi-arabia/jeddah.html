Jeddah

Jeddah served as the Red Sea gateway for Mecca pilgrims and Indian Ocean trade for centuries. Coral-stone houses with wooden roshan balconies in Al-Balad contrast with modern corniche development, recording Hejazi merchant architecture and contemporary Saudi urban growth. Corniche promenades and King Fahd Fountain extend Hejazi harbour life along the Red Sea shipping lanes.

Fakieh Aquarium

Fakieh Aquarium is a must-visit destination for those seeking an immersive experience with marine life. Located in Jeddah, Saudi Arabia, it is the only public aquarium in the country and has over 200 species of sea creatures from the Red Sea. Visitors can marvel at sharks, groupers, string rays, napoleon wrasse, sea horses, green turtles, and more. The aquarium plans to expand its collection to include even more fascinating underwater residents like the sea dragon.
