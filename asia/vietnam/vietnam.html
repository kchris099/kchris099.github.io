Vietnam

Vietnam developed through imperial dynasties centred at Huế, French colonial administration from the mid-19th century, and modern independence after 1945. Temples, colonial boulevards, markets, and war memorials in Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh City reflect riverine commerce, Confucian institutions, and national unification.
