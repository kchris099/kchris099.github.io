Explore Doha

A Brief History

Doha is an incredibly ambitious, rapidly expanding futuristic metropolis beautifully wrapped around a spectacular, sweeping turquoise bay. The city is highly globally recognized for its incredibly stunning, world-class Islamic art museums, deeply authentic, masterfully rebuilt traditional souqs, and highly innovative skyscraper architecture. It beautifully provides an exceptionally safe, deeply luxurious window into the modern Middle East.
