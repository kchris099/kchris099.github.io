Explore Izmir

A Brief History

Known historically as Smyrna, Izmir was a fabulously wealthy, cosmopolitan trade center of the Ottoman Empire. Following catastrophic fires during the 1922 Greco-Turkish War, the city was aggressively modernized, rising from the ashes as the fiercely progressive anchor of the Aegean coast. Visitors today can clearly trace the intricate layers of this unique heritage throughout the preserved streets and monuments.

Top Sights & Activities

₺30

Izmir Wildlife Park

Izmir Wildlife Park is a large zoo featuring animals from Africa, Asia, Europe, and the Americas. It's a popular destination for animal enthusiasts as it houses 2,700 animals from 134 different species. The park offers free admission and provides opportunities to observe wild animals, predators, and various bird species.

Free

İzmir Agora Mall

Izmir Agora Mall is a modern 2-story shopping center located in the historic center of Izmir, close to the Kemeralti Market. It is situated on the ancient Roman site known as Agora of Smyrna, offering visitors a chance to admire the remains of the ancient city. The mall features brand-name retail stores, a food court, and kid-friendly amenities.

₺200

İZULAŞ A.Ş. İZMİR BALÇOVA TELEFERİK

İZULAŞ A.Ş. İZMİR BALÇOVA TELEFERİK sits within the İZULAŞ A.Ş. İZMİR BALÇOVA TELEFERİK area of Izmir, documented in local heritage registers for its civic or cultural role. Visitors approach from the main access road and combine the visit with neighbouring heritage buildings and public spaces in the district.

Free

İnciraltı Kent Ormanı

İnciraltı Kent Ormanı is a hidden gem in Izmir, offering a serene and green escape by the seaside. It's perfect for walking, running, or cycling under the shade of trees with the sound of waves nearby. Locals call it 'heavenly garden' for its tranquil atmosphere. Accessible via tram/metro or ferry, this park boasts mature Eucalyptus and mulberry trees, with a quieter side near the red bridge.

Free

Güzelyali Park

Güzelyalı Park is a pleasant, coastal public park located in the Konak district of Izmir. Situated along the waterfront promenade, the park features wide walking paths, green lawns, and excellent views of the Gulf of Izmir. It is a popular, relaxed environment for evening strolls, family outings, and enjoying the sea breeze in the vibrant neighborhood.
