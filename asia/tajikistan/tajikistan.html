Tajikistan

Tajikistan covers the Pamir and Alay mountains where Persian-speaking communities traded along Silk Road passes. Part of the Emirate of Bukhara and later the Soviet Union until independence in 1991, it preserves Ismaili shrines, Pamiri villages, and planned capitals linked to cotton and hydropower.
