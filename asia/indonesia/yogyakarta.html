Yogyakarta

The Sultanate of Ngayogyakarta Hadiningrat, founded in the eighteenth century, still rules a special region of Indonesia from the kraton. Taman Sari's water castle and Malioboro's markets grew beside that court. Proximity to Borobudur and Prambanan links the living Javanese monarchy to older Hindu-Buddhist temple states. Monthly wayang and gamelan court performances keep palace arts public beyond museum display.
