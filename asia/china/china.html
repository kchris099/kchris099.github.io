China

China's recorded history stretches across three millennia of imperial capitals, treaty-port trade, and 20th-century republican and socialist state-building. Ming city walls, Tang grid streets, Bund facades, and restored temple districts in Beijing and Xi'an show how dynastic reform and maritime commerce shaped East Asia's most populous civilization.
