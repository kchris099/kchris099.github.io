Manila

Miguel López de Legazpi founded Spanish Manila in 1571 over a Muslim polity, and the galleon trade linked it to Acapulco. World War II combat devastated Intramuros; the rebuilt walls and San Agustín still stand for that colonial entrepôt on Manila Bay. Rizal Park stages national memory a short walk from the ruined and restored walled city. Binondo's Chinatown claims continuity as one of Asia's oldest Chinese quarters.
