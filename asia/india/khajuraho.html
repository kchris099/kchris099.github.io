Khajuraho

Flourishing between the 10th and 12th centuries under the Chandela dynasty, Khajuraho was a religious capital. Abandoned to the jungle and rediscovered in the 19th century, its surviving sandstone temples are globally known for their detailed, expressive sculptures. Visitors today can clearly trace the detailed layers of this unique heritage throughout the preserved streets and monuments.

Raneh Falls is a dramatic natural waterfall located on the Ken River, near Khajuraho. The river has carved a deep, dramatic canyon through pure crystalline granite in varying shades of pink, red, and grey.
