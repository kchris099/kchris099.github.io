Mysore

The Wodeyar dynasty made Mysore their capital for centuries, interrupted by Haider Ali and Tipu Sultan, then restored under British paramountcy. The Indo-Saracenic palace of 1912 and Dasara processions are the public face of that courtly Mysore state in southern Karnataka. Tipu's summer palace in wooden Indo-Islamic style survives from the brief sultanate interval.
