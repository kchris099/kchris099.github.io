Bangalore

Established as a mud fort in 1537, Bangalore evolved into a major British military cantonment due to its favorable climate. Post-independence investments in heavy industry and research laid the foundation for its explosive change into India's premier technology hub. Visitors today can clearly trace the detailed layers of this unique heritage throughout the preserved streets and monuments.
