Munnar

Developed in the late 19th century by British planters, Munnar was transformed from dense forest into sweeping tea estates. The colonial-era infrastructure and long-running plantation economy completely reshaped the landscape, establishing a lasting legacy in the Western Ghats. Visitors today can clearly trace the detailed layers of this unique heritage throughout the preserved streets and monuments.
