Oman Guide

Oman's Indian Ocean ports preserved Ibadi imamates, Portuguese coastal forts, and modern sultanate planning along the Arabian Sea. Muscat's white harbour mosques, Nizwa's Friday market, and Dhofar frankincense trails reflect maritime trade with East Africa and India in a Gulf kingdom distinct from its oil-rich neighbours.
