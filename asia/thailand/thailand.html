Thailand

Thailand's history runs from early riverine and Mon-Khmer settlements through the Sukhothai and Ayutthaya kingdoms to Bangkok's Chakri dynasty and the modern constitutional state. Temple cities, royal capitals, teak and trading towns, and southern island communities reveal how Buddhist, Chinese, Malay, and regional cultures shaped Siam and its successor nation.
