Thailand Guide

Thailand's Chakri dynasty made Bangkok the capital in 1782 after earlier Siamese kingdoms united much of the Chao Phraya basin. Theravada Buddhism, royal institutions, and river trade shaped palace compounds, temples, markets, and transport corridors across the country's central lowlands.
